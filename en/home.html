Chinazi.wiki strives to document China's rising nationalism and the fight against it, with an emphasis on the intersection of Chinese politics and internet culture.

Editing Chinazi.wiki

Anyone is welcome to edit this site. Click on the button on the top-right corner to register/login. Due to a system bug, confirmation emails aren't working at the moment. New accounts will be manually activated instead, which should take around 12 hours to 1 day.

Content index

Most of the content on this site is divided into the following 4 categories

  • NationalismDb: a register of acts of nationalism against those who were perceived to have "hurt the feelings of the Chinese people."
  • Dictionary: a documentation of internet slang that frequently arises in politically-charged discussions in China.
  • Encyclopedia Winnica: a knowledgebase of memes, trivia, and nicknames of Xi Jinping
  • KOL Recycling Bin: an archive of inflammatory ethnonationalist articles and their authors.
